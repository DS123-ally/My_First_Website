What is Energy?

Energy is the ability to do work. It powers our homes, industries, and transportation. Common sources include fossil fuels, nuclear, and renewables like solar or wind.

How to Conserve Energy

  • Switch off lights and appliances when not in use.
  • Use energy-efficient LED bulbs and appliances.
  • Insulate your home to reduce heating/cooling needs.
  • Use public transport, cycle, or walk whenever possible.
  • Install solar panels if feasible.
