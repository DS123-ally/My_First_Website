Why Conserve Water?

Water is a vital and limited natural resource. Conserving water ensures access for future generations, supports life on Earth, and helps maintain ecological balance.

Steps to Conserve Water

  • Fix leaking taps and pipes immediately.
  • Use water-efficient appliances.
  • Turn off taps while brushing or shaving.
  • Collect and use rainwater wisely.
  • Spread awareness in your community.
  • Practice greywater reuse for gardening.

  • Improves resilience to droughts and climate change.
  • Promotes sustainable development.
